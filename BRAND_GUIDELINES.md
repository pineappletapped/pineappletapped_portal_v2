Pineapple Tapped - Brand Guidelines
===================================

1. Introduction
----------------
Welcome to the brand guidelines for Pineapple Tapped. This document is the guide to our visual and verbal identity. It ensures that we present ourselves to the world with clarity, confidence, and consistency across every platform.

Our mission is to empower businesses to navigate the digital landscape, build a powerful online presence, and create meaningful connections with their audience.

Our Methodology: Signpost - Showcase - Engage
---------------------------------------------
Our core methodology is the foundation of our brand and our process. It's how we deliver value and drive results for our clients.

* **SIGNPOST:** We provide clarity and strategic direction, mapping out the path to success.
* **SHOWCASE:** We build beautiful, professional platforms and create compelling visuals that demand attention.
* **ENGAGE:** We connect our clients with their audience through strategic marketing and compelling content.

This framework should inform all of our communications and visual expressions.

2. The Logo
-----------
Our logo is the most immediate representation of Pineapple Tapped. It is a confident, professional, and modern mark that is both memorable and meaningful. It combines a refined, geometric pineapple icon with a strong, clean wordmark. The upward-pointing leaves of the pineapple signify growth and ambition.

### Primary Logo
This is the main logo and should be used in most instances. It features the icon and the full wordmark.

### Logo Variations
To ensure flexibility across different media and backgrounds, we have several logo variations.

| Variation | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Wordmark** | For use when the brand name needs to be prominent and space is limited horizontally. |
| **Icon** | For use as a favicon, social media profile picture, or when the brand is already well-established in the context. |
| **Monochrome** | For use on backgrounds where the full-color logo would not have enough contrast, or for single-color printing. |

### Clearspace & Sizing
To ensure our logo's integrity and visibility, always maintain a minimum clearspace around it. This space should be equal to the height of the pineapple icon's crown.

The logo should not be reproduced at a width smaller than 120 pixels in digital applications or 1.5 inches in print.

### Incorrect Usage
Maintaining brand consistency is crucial. Never alter the logo in any of the following ways:

* Do not stretch or distort the logo.
* Do not change the logo's colors outside of the approved monochrome versions.
* Do not place the logo on a visually cluttered or low-contrast background.
* Do not rearrange the elements of the logo.
* Do not add drop shadows or other visual effects.

3. Color Palette
----------------
Our color palette is bold, professional, and engaging. It creates a high-contrast, energetic, and trustworthy feel.

### Primary Palette
These colors should be the most prominent in all of our visual communications.

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Vibrant Orange** | `#E8793B` | The primary brand accent. Represents energy, creativity, and action. Use for calls-to-action, key highlights, and the logo icon. |
| **Navy Blue** | `#215696` | Our core brand color. Represents professionalism, trust, and strategy. Use for headlines, body text, and dark backgrounds. |

### Secondary Palette
These colors support our primary palette, providing balance and flexibility.

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Light Blue** | `#89CFF0` | A secondary accent for subsidiary information, icons, or subtle background elements. |
| **Light Gray** | `#F0F4F8` | Clean and modern backgrounds, providing space for our content to breathe. |
| **Pure White** | `#FFFFFF` | For text on dark backgrounds and for negative space. |

4. Typography
-------------
Our typography is clean, modern, and highly legible. It reflects our clarity and professionalism. We use a single, versatile font family to maintain consistency and hierarchy.

### Primary Typeface: Poppins
Poppins is a geometric sans-serif typeface that is professional, friendly, and easy to read on screens. We use a range of weights to establish a clear hierarchy.

Typographic Hierarchy
---------------------

| Style | Usage |
|-------|-------|
| **H1 - Page Titles:** Poppins Bold, 40px | Used to SIGNPOST the main topic of a page. |
| **H2 - Section Headings:** Poppins SemiBold, 32px | Used to SHOWCASE key sections of content. |
| **H3 - Sub-Headings:** Poppins Medium, 20px | Used for organizing content within a section. |
| **Body Text:** Poppins Regular, 16px | The primary text for paragraphs. Designed for readability to ENGAGE the user. |
| **Buttons & CTAs:** Poppins SemiBold, 14px (ALL CAPS) | For all interactive elements. |

5. Imagery & Graphics
---------------------
Our visual style should always be professional, authentic, and human-centric.

### Photography
* **Style:** Bright, clean, and high-quality.
* **Subject:** Showcase collaboration, strategy sessions, and technology in use. Feature real people in professional environments. Avoid generic, posed stock photography.
* **Tone:** Should feel optimistic, professional, and engaging.

### Graphic Elements
Simple, clean lines and geometric patterns derived from the logo can be used to add visual interest to backgrounds and marketing materials. These elements should be subtle and support the content, not overwhelm it.

6. Voice & Tone
---------------
Our voice is how we sound. Our tone is the emotional inflection we apply to that voice in different situations.

### Our Voice is:
* **Strategic:** We are experts who think critically and provide clear direction.
* **Professional:** We are reliable, trustworthy, and polished in our communication.
* **Clear:** We avoid jargon and communicate complex ideas in a simple, understandable way.

### Our Tone is:
* **Confident:** We are assured in our expertise and the value we provide.
* **Approachable:** We are friendly and easy to work with. We are partners, not just vendors.
* **Engaging:** We are passionate about what we do and aim to inspire action and connection.

### Example Application
* **Website Copy (Confident & Clear):** "We build powerful websites with integrated client portals that streamline your workflow and elevate your brand."
* **Social Media (Approachable & Engaging):** "What's the one thing you wish your website did better? Let's talk about it in the comments! 👇"
